Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/12/18

Your privacy

At Milestone Drones we value your privacy as much as we do our own, so we’re committed to storing any information that we collect securely, and in compliance with the GDPR. We will never misuse or give anyone else access to your personal information.


Information we collect

User submitted data:

When you contact us, we store certain information for our records.

Our contact form requires that you submit your full name and email address, and this is necessary so that we can respond to your enquiry.

Additionally, the IP address that submitted the contact form will be temporarily stored, solely to help us identify bots, spam and other malicious activity. On the subject of spam, we will never send you any, or give your details to people who will.

Any personal information you send to us via direct email is entirely up to you, and this information may be temporarily stored if there is valid reason to do so, e.g. if the information is relevant to an ongoing or prospective project.

Should we communicate via telephone, your number will be stored only for as long as is necessary. It will not be shared with any third parties or used to further marketing endeavours.

All user submitted data and personal information is stored safely and locally within our CRM system.

Google Analytics data:

Like most websites, we also gather anonymised statistical data from Google Analytics. Because this data is anonymised, it is impossible for anyone to derive your personal or private information.

We use these analytics to learn about website traffic patterns, how users navigate the site, and the kinds of devices that are most commonly used to view the site. With this information we care only to enhance the utility and performance of our website, and deem this to be a 'legitimate use' as outlined within the GDPR.

Without these basic analytics it is difficult for us to plan and optimise our content. For example, interactive 3D models are not easily manipulated on mobile devices, and are slow to load. Knowing the percentage of users who browse on mobile devices allows us to tailor our content and subsequently improve the general user experience for the majority of our visitors.

These analytics also shine light on how users are navigating the site, and these insights help us to ensure that users are achieving their objectives, and not missing important information.

Furthermore, these analytics enable us to: estimate server load and performance so that we can keep our site operational during busy periods, schedule maintenance at non-disruptive times, estimate the reach and effectiveness of our content so that we can further improve it, and reduce the likelihood of cyber threats, spam and general misuse.

All data collected by Google Analytics is anonymised, and stored at Google’s data centers in the United States. It is accessible only to trusted senior team members via the Google Analytics platform.

Google Analytics operates using cookies. Cookies are tiny text files stored on your computer, and the data that a particular cookie contains can only be accessed by the entity that created it (Google Analytics in our case). You are free to block and delete browser cookies at any time to prevent this anonymous data from being collected. Pre-existing cookies on your system will eventually expire and be removed of their own accord if you take no action.

To be explicit, we take no interest in building user profiles that facilitate targeted marketing. We find this practice invasive. The sole purpose of our anonymised data collection is to improve the utility and reliability of our website and services.


Google’s privacy policy: http://www.google.com/privacy.html


Please note that you can opt-out of all Google Analytics tracking using Google's opt-out tool: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en


Changes to our policy

Policy updates will become effective the moment we publish them to our website.


Your rights

You have the right to view any information that we store about you, and to request that this information be safely deleted. We will respect this right without hesitation. Please get in touch if you are concerned about your privacy.


Contacting us

For matters concerning your privacy you can contact us via email, telephone or the contact form on our website. We will be happy to help.
